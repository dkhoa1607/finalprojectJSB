Lunar New Year

Lunar New Year is the beginning of a calendar year whose months are moon cycles, based on the lunar calendar or lunisolar calendar. Lunar New Year was first celebrated in the region of the Korean peninsula. The Lunar New Year as a celebration is observed by numerous cultures

In Japan
In China
